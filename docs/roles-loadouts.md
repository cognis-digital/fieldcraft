# Roles & loadouts

> Educational/preparedness gear planning. Build your real-life character: pick roles, get kit + skills + references.

Compose with `fieldcraft loadout <role>,<role> --tier edc|24h|72h|sustainment`.

## Survivalist  (`survivalist`)

Water, fire, shelter, signaling — the core priorities of survival.

**Skills:** water procurement & purification, fire-craft, shelter-building, signaling, foraging safety

**edc kit:** lighter + ferro rod; fixed-blade knife; water filter straw; cordage (paracord); emergency blanket; whistle
**72h kit:** water filter + tablets; metal canteen/cup; tarp + stakes; sleeping bag/bivy; headlamp + spares; fire kit (multiple methods); signal mirror; first-aid kit

**Learn:** survival, food-water, environment  ·  **Cognis:** labforge

## Medic / TCC-aware first responder  (`medic`)

Hemorrhage control and field first aid (Stop the Bleed / TCCC-informed).

**Skills:** MARCH assessment, tourniquet application, wound packing, airway basics, wilderness first aid

**edc kit:** tourniquet (CoTCCC-recommended); compressed gauze; gloves; trauma shears; marker
**72h kit:** IFAK (TQ, hemostatic gauze, chest seals, NPA, pressure bandage); burn dressing; splint (SAM); OTC meds; first-aid manual; PPE

**Learn:** medical-tccc

## Navigator  (`navigator`)

Move accurately with map, compass, and terrain — no batteries required.

**Skills:** map reading, compass + declination, MGRS/UTM, pace count, terrain association

**edc kit:** baseplate compass; area map (waterproof); pace beads; pencil
**72h kit:** topo maps + protractor; GPS + spare batteries; altimeter/watch; map case

**Learn:** land-nav, mil-reference  ·  **Cognis:** labforge

## Comms operator  (`comms`)

Keep a group connected with a PACE plan and licensed radio.

**Skills:** PACE planning, radio operation & etiquette, antennas/propagation basics, phonetics & prowords

**edc kit:** GMRS/FRS handheld; freq/PACE card; spare batteries
**72h kit:** programmable HT (licensed); roll-up antenna; notepad; power bank; AM/FM/weather radio

**Learn:** comms  ·  **Cognis:** edgemesh

## Rucker / load-bearer  (`rucker`)

Move weight over distance without getting injured.

**Skills:** load packing & distribution, foot care, pacing, progressive conditioning

**edc kit:** broken-in boots; good socks (wool); blister kit
**sustainment kit:** rucksack/frame; load ~1/3 bodyweight max; hydration; weight plate/sandbag; dry bags; trekking poles

**Learn:** rucking

## Logistics / sustainment  (`logistics`)

Plan food, water, power, and supply for days, not hours.

**Skills:** ration/calorie planning, water storage, power/energy, inventory & rotation

**72h kit:** 3 days food (~2000+ kcal/day); water 1 gal/person/day; stove + fuel; power bank/solar; cash; documents copy
**sustainment kit:** bulk food + rotation log; water containers + purification; fuel store; generator/solar; spares

**Learn:** food-water, preparedness  ·  **Cognis:** labforge

## Scout / observer  (`scout`)

Situational awareness, observation, and reporting (descriptive).

**Skills:** observation & reporting (SALUTE/SITREP), terrain analysis (OAKOC), low-signature movement, optics use

**edc kit:** binoculars/monocular; notebook + pencil; watch; map
**72h kit:** spotting optics; range card/notebook; camera; comms (see comms role)

**Learn:** land-nav, mil-reference  ·  **Cognis:** maritimeint, geoaoi
